Privacy Policy for "Clear Cache" Extension

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Clear Cache" Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles user data. The Extension is developed by [Your Name or Company] and is available via the Chrome Web Store.

2. Data Collection

The Extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data about users. It does not access or send:

  • Browsing history
  • Cookies or cache content
  • IP addresses, device information, or location
  • Any form of user input or activity logs

3. Local Data Usage

The Extension uses the following permissions to function locally on your device:

  • browsingData — to delete cache, cookies, and history when requested by the user.
  • storage — to save your cleanup preferences (e.g., “clear cache only”) between sessions.
  • notifications — to show a brief confirmation after cleanup.
  • webNavigation — to update the icon state after page reloads (for UI feedback).

All data remains on your device. No data is sent to servers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.
